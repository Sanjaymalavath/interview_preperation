5.Difference between block and inline?

Block: This elements always begins from new line, and it extends from left to right.
It takes full width available on the screen.Browser automatically adds some space before and after the element.
Ex : div , h1 to h6 , p , ol , ul , table ,etc. .

Inline: Does not begin on new line. It only takes the space that is necessary for it .
Ex: span , img , input , a , label etc..
